Next-Generation Intelligent Semiconductor Energy Engineering and Analysis Laboratory

Semiconductor materials, device engineering, and practical energy applications.

i-SEAL is an interdisciplinary laboratory at Kyungpook National University working on semiconductor materials, characterization, interface engineering, bandgap and carrier control, and device prototyping for real-world applications.
